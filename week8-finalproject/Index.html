CatEye is promoting an urban art gallery ride, taking urban riders through the concrete jungle from installation to installation. Promoting the cities best urban street artists in their respective towns. We‘re partnering with local street artist to curate the first route starting in Denver, and soon after in London. The artists will also lead the way with his CatEye light, navigating riders safely and securely through the city.
